Overview

OneZeroEight.ai uses intelligent agents to connect artists with verified playlist curators for transparent, Spotify-compliant music promotion. We serve all artists — independent, label, and AI-generated alike.

Our Mission

Build ethical infrastructure for music promotion. No bots, no fake streams, no black boxes — just real curators, real placements, real results.

Key Features

  • 4,000+ Verified Curators: Real playlist curators with 500+ followers, growing daily
  • Bot-Free Network: Every curator verified. Real engagement only.
  • Full Transparency: See every curator who reviews your track and their feedback
  • SUTRA-Powered: Earn and spend tokens across the ecosystem
  • 100% Spotify Safe: All promotion is TOS-compliant

How It Works

  1. Submit Your Track: Add your Spotify link with genre tags and artist info
  2. Choose Your Campaign: Select the tier that fits your goals
  3. Agents Activate: Our intelligent agents match you with curators in your genre
  4. Receive Reviews: Curators provide honest feedback within 24-48 hours
  5. Get Placements: Accepted tracks are added to real, active playlists
  6. Earn Rewards: Get SUTRA tokens for quality submissions and placements

Quick Start Guide

For Artists

  1. Visit music.onezeroeight.ai
  2. Click "Start Campaign" and fill out the form
  3. Select your campaign tier (we recommend Growth for first-timers)
  4. Submit payment and wait for curator matches
  5. Track your results in real-time

For Curators

  1. Apply at our curator application form
  2. We verify your playlist (500+ followers required)
  3. Once approved, you'll receive track submissions
  4. Add tracks that fit your playlist, keep them for 30+ days
  5. Earn 50 SUTRA per placement — tokens accrue in your account

Running Campaigns

When you submit a campaign, your track goes through our matching algorithm to find curators who are most likely to appreciate and place your music.

Pro Tip

Include accurate genre tags and a compelling artist bio. Curators use this information to decide if your track fits their playlist.

Campaign Lifecycle

  1. Submission (Day 0): You submit your track and payment
  2. Matching (Day 0-1): We match you with relevant curators
  3. Review Period (Day 1-7): Curators listen and provide feedback
  4. Placement (Day 2-14): Accepted tracks get added to playlists
  5. Monitoring (Ongoing): We track playlist status for 30 days

How to Apply (Curators)

Requirements

  • Spotify playlist with 500+ followers
  • Real engagement (no bot-inflated playlists)
  • Open to reviewing music across genres
  • Commitment to 30-day minimum placements

Application Process

  1. Fill out the curator application form
  2. We verify your playlist metrics (48-72 hours)
  3. If approved, you receive onboarding email
  4. Start receiving track submissions and earning SUTRA

Reviewing Tracks (Curators)

When you receive a track submission:

  1. Listen to the full track
  2. Evaluate fit for your playlist's genre and vibe
  3. Accept tracks that fit — add to your playlist for minimum 30 days
  4. Decline tracks that don't fit (no penalty)

Quality Standards

We're building a bot-free network. Curators who inflate stats, remove tracks early, or game the system will be removed from the program.
