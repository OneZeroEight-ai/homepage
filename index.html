About OneZeroEight.ai

Our Mission

At OneZeroEight.ai (OZEN), we foster a sustainable ecosystem where ethical alignment and beneficial outcomes are incentivized through blockchain-powered services and the SUTRA token.

We're pioneering practical solutions for AI ethics verification, governance, data integrity, and trust systems that provide immediate value while building toward our long-term vision.

Our Approach

We combine cutting-edge blockchain technology with ethical frameworks inspired by timeless philosophical traditions to create verifiable, transparent, and incentive-aligned systems.

By focusing on practical applications first, we're building a foundation of trust, users, and proven technology that will support our vision of a comprehensive ethical AI ecosystem.
